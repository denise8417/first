My Recipe How to make Korean Kimchi Stew with Pork Belly

Description

Korean Kimchi Stew is a Korean soup dish that consists of kimchi as the main ingredient. Kimchi is fermented cabbage with other various vegetables and is made with a variety of seasonings and sauces for flavor, these ingredients include gochugaru (red pepper flakes/powder), spring onions, garlic, ginger, and jeotgal, etc.

Ingredients for two servings

  • Kimchi (preferably aged,for sour taste)
  • 1/4 cup kimchi brine
  • 1/2 pound of pork belly (this can be substituted with something else for a vegetarian version such as more tofu mushrooms, etc.)
  • 1/2 package of firm tofu
  • green onion (preferred amount)
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons gochugaru (Korean red pepper flakes)
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang (red pepper paste)
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 2 cups water

Steps

  1. step one: Add the kimchi and kimchi brine to a shallow pot. Add pork and onion
  2. step two: Add the green onion
  3. step three: Add salt, sugar, hot pepper flakes, and hot pepper paste. Drizzle sesame oil over and add the 2 cups of water
  4. step four: Cover and cook for 10 minutes on medium high heat
  5. step five: Spread the seasonings around then add sliced firm tofu on top
  6. step six: Cook for an additional 10-15 minutues
  7. step 7: Finally, add the chopped green onion and serve with white rice
